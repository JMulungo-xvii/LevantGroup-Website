About Us

Levant Group Ltd develops and implements agribusiness projects, solutions to address development challenges related to water, sanitation, and hygien, public and private infrastructure projects including real estate development, solutions to improve service delivery, research to inform better development practice and provides technical assistance services in the areas of economic, social and organizational development.

Our Values:

Levant Group Ltd is guided by the following values that define who we are, what we stand for, and how we behave.

Integrity

We have a strong commitment to guide our operations with high ethical standards and rules.

Excellence

We are strongly committed to excellence and always strive for the highest technical and professional standards

Accountability:

We are accountable to our clients and those affected by our operations.

Diversity

We empower people who work or interact with us by respecting and appreciating what makes them different, allowing for the exploration of their differences in a safe, positive, and nurturing environment.
